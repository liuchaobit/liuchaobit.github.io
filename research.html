Research

My research focuses on developing innovative approaches to modeling and control systems, with particular emphasis on geometric methods, advanced control techniques, navigation planning, and AI applications in engineering education.

Geometric Modeling and Control

My work in geometric modeling and control involves utilizing Lie groups to describe system dynamics, with a particular emphasis on designing coordinate-free controllers. This approach offers a powerful framework for expressing complex geometric relationships within a system, facilitating a deeper understanding of its dynamics.

By employing Lie group theory, a coordinate-independent perspective is achieved, enhancing the efficiency and versatility of control strategies. This innovative methodology is instrumental in addressing intricate challenges in system control without relying on conventional coordinate-based approaches.

Advanced Control

Advanced control involves nonlinear control algorithms and learning control, addressing complex system dynamics and real-world challenges. Nonlinear control deals with nonlinear system characteristics, using advanced mathematical tools for systems like robots and aircraft.

Learning control improves performance through real-time adaptation, employing techniques like reinforcement learning and neural networks for flexible responses to changing environments. The combination of nonlinear control and learning control provides innovative solutions for modern complex systems, advancing control theory and applications.

Navigation and Planning

Navigation and Planning, with a focus on AI applications and SLAM technology, has transformed how autonomous systems operate. AI enhances decision-making through advanced algorithms, while SLAM allows devices to map and navigate real-time environments.

The synergy of AI and SLAM reshapes autonomous systems, enabling them to navigate complex surroundings effectively.

AI in Engineering Education

AI in Engineering Education emphasizes personalized learning through the use of AI technologies. By employing machine learning algorithms, AI tailors educational experiences to individual students, analyzing their performance and adapting content accordingly.

This personalized approach enhances engagement and effectiveness, transforming Engineering Education into a dynamic and adaptive system that equips students with the skills for success in their academic and professional journeys.
